  • Vail, Colorado

    Vail is another popular ski resort in Colorado, known for its vast terrain and excellent facilities. The resort has over 5,200 acres of skiable terrain, as well as a wide range of dining and lodging options.

  • Park City Mountain Resort, Utah

    Park City Mountain Resort is the largest ski resort in the United States, with over 7,300 acres of skiable terrain. It also offers plenty of non-skiing activities, including an alpine coaster, zip lines, and a snow park.

  • Whistler Blackcomb, British Columbia

    While technically not in the United States, Whistler Blackcomb is a must-visit destination for any serious skier. With over 8,100 acres of skiable terrain, it's the largest ski resort in North America. The resort also has a lively village with plenty of dining and nightlife options.
